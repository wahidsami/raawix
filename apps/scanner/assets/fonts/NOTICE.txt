Bundled Noto fonts (SIL Open Font License 1.1) for PDF export:

- NotoSans-Regular.ttf, NotoSans-Bold.ttf — https://github.com/notofonts/latin-greek-cyrillic
- NotoNaskhArabic-Regular.ttf, NotoNaskhArabic-Bold.ttf — https://github.com/notofonts/arabic

Used for (1) @font-face embedding in Playwright HTML→PDF and (2) pdf-lib fallback when Chromium PDF fails.
